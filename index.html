NICK DRAKE

PINK MOON

A living, interactive tribute to English singer-songwriter Nick Drake (1948–1974), built from the surreal artwork of his final studio album, Pink Moon, released on Island Records in 1972. The cover's teacup, leaf, ghost face, postage stamp, moon and shell are animated in the browser and can be dragged and moved around. Listen to the full album below.
